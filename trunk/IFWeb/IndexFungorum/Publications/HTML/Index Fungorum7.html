Nomenclatural novelties : Paul D. Bridge & Kevin A. Hughes

Mortierella signyensis K. Voigt, P.M. Kirk & Bridge sp. nov.
    IF550057

With an ITS sequence (GenBank JQ693160) that is distinct from other members of the gamsii/elongata clade, deviating in the ITS1 region from other species in the clade; with a 94-97% similarity. With a sister group relationship to a possibly polyphyletic clade containing Mortierella sclerotiella (basal; GenBank HQ63031, ex type), M. cogitans (GenBank HQ630281, ex type) and M. acrotona (GenBank HQ630328, ex type).


    Holotype IMI 398111.
